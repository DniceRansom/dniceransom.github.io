Mr. Sample man

The hardworking man

Image

Here's a time line of Mr. Sample Man's life:

•1950: Born in Nigeria, Africa.

•1980: Leaves his family farm business to attend University of Nigeria.

•1983: Had to stop school and save up more money.

•1986: Continued his University education.

•1988: Finishes university and prepares for service.

•1989: Went for his youth service.

•1990: Finishes his youth service and takes a job in his home state Forestry service.

•1995: Marries wife of 27 years Naomi Ruth. Gets laid off due to budget cuts. Inspired by his loving friend, he returns to school to study about breeding pest-resistant plants.

•1998: Enroll in local meetups where he learnt about computer programming.

•2000: Receives a master degree in Genetic and Plant Pathology.

•2002: Finishes learning computer programming.

•2003: Accepts a 100 percent salary increase and headed a new plant pathology program. Over the next couple of years, his team breeds 2000 different strains of disease resistant wheat plant.

•2005: Discovers a way to grow wheat twice each season, doubling wheat yields.

•2007: Mr. Sample man story is to be continued...
